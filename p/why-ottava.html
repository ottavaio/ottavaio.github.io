Why Ottava?

Alright, folks, gather around! In a world where spreadsheets and databases can be a real snooze-fest, Ottava is here to set your data dreams on fire. We're not just software; we're a game-changer, a data revolution like no other. But hey, have you ever wondered how Ottava came to life? Get ready for a behind-the-scenes journey that's more inspiring than you'd expect.

Who Are We? What is Our Story and Why Do We Understand Spreadsheets That Much?

In 2005, Ottava's journey began, inspired by the pioneer ZK framework, a game-changer in web UI development. We started with simple UI components and gradually evolved to create ZK Spreadsheet. More than a decade of dedication to the spreadsheet domain led us to Keikai, the next-gen web spreadsheet framework.

Our journey wasn't just about technical expertise; it was also about building meaningful relationships with a diverse clientele. We've proudly served Fortune 500 companies, delivering excellence, while also becoming the preferred choice for individuals and small teams seeking a white-label Google Sheets alternative. Our mission is to empower users to safeguard their ideas and share expertise with confidence.

But our story doesn't end with spreadsheets. We've excelled in the SaaS platform realm, with Quire, our Project Management Software, faithfully serving thousands of companies for an incredible decade. Join us on this exciting journey, where innovation and client satisfaction drive everything we do.

Ottava: Where Data Entry Gets a Makeover

Alright, let's dive a little deeper into what makes Ottava truly stand out. When it comes to data entry, Ottava isn't just different; it's a game-changer. You see, other spreadsheet-based apps often have you entering data in a tabular format, one data point per row. Sure, it works well for statistics and crunching numbers, but let's be honest - it can be a real headache when you need to pivot your data.

While other apps often require you to work with un-pivot tables, Ottava offers a refreshingly different approach. Our goal is to not only simplify data entry but to make it incredibly intuitive. Picture this: you input your data into an already-pivoted table, and suddenly, it's like a breath of fresh air. You're greeted with an instant, crystal-clear overview of your data.

The outcome? Data entry becomes a breeze, and you can bid farewell to those troublesome human errors that tend to sneak into the picture, especially when managing complex spreadsheets. Ottava doesn't just change the game; it completely transforms your data interaction experience. Say hello to data entry that's as smooth as a hot knife through butter, all thanks to Ottava's innovative and superior approach.
